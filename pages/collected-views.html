I am a British expat who has lived and worked in Ukraine since 2017. In the last four years I have needed both private and public Ukrainian services for a number of medical issues. Having experience of medical assistance in the UK and a number of European countries I am well placed to comment on the help I have received in Ukraine. The range of services available is second to none. Private hospitals and clinics offer modern diagnostic and treatment options at reasonable prices, particularly in comparison with Europe and North America. At the same time public hospitals, particularly in Kyiv and other major cities, have benefitted from Government and international funding and training and represent the best option for major surgery and costly diagnostic services such as MRI and CT scans. In my case, I went to my usual private provider with, what I thought, was a fairly minor complaint. My doctor became concerned about my irregular heartbeat. He immediately referred me to the public cardiac hospital where, four days later, it was identified I had a potentially serious heart problem and, three days after that, I was admitted and the condition was successfully dealt with. Unlike in many other countries, private and public providers here in Ukraine cooperate closely rather than compete. This ensures that the treatments offered can be fully and swiftly implemented. I would have no hesitation in recommending anyone who has a medical issue to consider Ukraine for treatment. You won’t regret it.

In parallel, I managed to get appointment with the gynaecologist, who having examined me gave me a referral for tests and to a mammologist. All procedures have taken just a week. And if we consider the financial component, then it cost times and times cheaper than if it had been done in Europe. In the future, I plan to go through a full check-up of my body and get appointment with the dentist. Definitely it will be cheaper, and most importantly, it will not require long waiting time for an appointment with a doctor and it will save my time and nerves!
