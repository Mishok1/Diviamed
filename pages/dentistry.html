OUR SERVICES

OUR SERVICES

We will work with you to develop an affordable, comprehensive plan and support package to ensure that your dental needs are fully addressed. The services offered by DiviaMed include:

Preliminary Examination– diagnosis along with the development of treatment plans utilizing the most modern equipment including 3D scans, endodontic microscopy, computer visualization and other devices as necessary.

Tooth Implantation- provision of an effective replacement for a failed tooth or teeth that will not only improve your appearance but also address other related problems such as speech, dental occlusion and digestion. The processes available can include tooth extraction, implant preparation and final implantation.

Tooth Restoration– the repair of teeth damaged by decay or trauma. These procedures can include partial filling or the use of crowns and veneers to reinstate the colour and shape of the damaged tooth using the latest techniques and composite materials.

Orthodontic Services– includes the diagnosis, prevention and correction of mal-positioned teeth and jaws through the fitting of brace, lingual or buccal bracket systems made from the most appropriate modern materials.

Orthopaedic Dental Services– the diagnosis and correction of teeth irregularities requiring the realignment or other modification of the jaws and skeletal system.

Endodontic Surgery– the location of small fractures or hidden canals previously undetected on X-rays during initial treatment. This can save your tooth through the removal of calcium deposits in root canals, treat damaged root surfaces or the surrounding bone of the tooth.

Pediatric Dentistry– children requiring dental treatment need careful and sympathetic handling, innovative treatments and attention to detail. DiviaMed have identified partners who have wide experience of these needs alongside the highest standards of dental treatment.
