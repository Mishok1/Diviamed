How to start

Step-by-step

  • You

    • register your account;
    • fill in the necessary form;
    • download all your or your child’s previous medical records and reports.

  • We

    • make a schedule of specialists within your expectations and in accordance with processed information of yours;
    • our doctors-partners review the medical records and answer within 48 hours with their treatment/rehabilitation plan;
    • provide constant feedback from the doctors and arrange your visit schedule;
    • meet you in Kyiv to help you to fulfil the visit schedule.

P.S. Upon request there can be arranged additional services. (Please see “Support SERVICES”)

Let’s do it together! Your success is our reputation!
