STEM CELL THERAPY

Ukrainian experts have been studying and developing stem cell treatments for over two decades. Stem cell therapy is a non-invasive treatment that replaces damaged cells within the body, reduces inflammation and supports the immune system. Stem cell therapies are a viable treatment option for a variety of medical conditions. Stem cell therapies have been used to treat autoimmune, inflammatory, neurological, orthopaedic conditions and traumatic injuries. Stem cells can be deployed systemically via IV or injected locally to target specific sites, depending on patient needs.

While stem cell therapy does not necessarily provide a cure, in most cases the treatments will sufficiently alleviate symptoms and delay the progress of a disease for long enough to significantly improve the patient’s quality of life.
