SUPPORT SERVICES

If this is your first visit to Ukraine, it could be easy to feel overwhelmed, lost and be uncertain about where to go and what to do next.

OUR SUPPORT SERVICES

DiviaMed can offer support to you for all aspects of your visit to ensure that your trip and stay is as comfortable as it can be. Here are just some of the areas in which we can help:

TRANSPORTATION

Kyiv is a modern, international city with a comprehensive public and private transport infrastructure including metro, tram, bus, taxi and limousine services. DiviaMed can arrange the following on your behalf:

  • Initial transfer to and from the airport;
  • Transfers to and from the medical facility;
  • Transport for leisure.

ACCOMMODATION

Kyiv offers a range of hotels and apartments to suit every budget. These are available for individuals, couples or families on a daily, weekly or longer basis at advantageous rates negotiated by DiviaMed.

LEISURE AND SIGHTSEEING

Kyiv is not only the capital of Ukraine, but an exciting and historic city, with something for every visitor. DiviaMed is able to recommend a tour programme, providing you all the information you need to navigate yourself around or even arrange guided tours with one of the many specialist companies that exist in Kyiv.

  • History and Culture – there is enough to keep you occupied for several days, including: cathedrals, monasteries, monuments, museums and art galleries.
  • Kyiv Opera House – DiviaMed can arrange tickets so that you can have the unique opportunity to view a classical or modern opera or the ballet in the baroque splendour of the Kyiv Opera House.;
  • Concerts and Music – if you want to experience something a little more lively, then there are any number of venues offering jazz, folk, rock and classical concerts, for which DiviaMed can arrange tickets, as well as musical and other entertainment in bars and nightclub.
  • For the Kids – there is plenty for them to do too, including the Kyiv Puppet Theatre, the Kyiv Zoo, indoor and outdoor play areas and entertainment complexes such as Kiddiland (a model town where children carry out various “jobs”).
  • Tours – if you’re feeling adventurous, tours can be arranged to Chernobyl, the Pirohovo historical village, Kievan Rus Park, River tours etc.
  • Restaurants – Kyiv offers a wide range of international, Ukrainian and ethnic restaurants at prices you can afford.

CHILD-MINDING

Should you wish to bring your family, you may need someone to care for your children while you are undergoing medical assessment or treatment or wish to participate in a leisure event alone. DiviaMed can arrange child care by qualified, English speaking child-minders on an hourly or daily basis. Depending on the season and your preferences, the child minder can look after your kids in your accommodation or take them to a place of entertainment.

PHOTOGRAPHIC MEMENTO

DiviaMed can arrange for a professional photographer to provide a photographic or video memento of your visit.

INTERPRETATION AND TRANSLATION

Translation of all your medical and legal documents can be provided by DiviaMed’s English speaking team. In addition, we can provide an interpreter to accompany you on both your medical or leisure visits.

VISAS

Visits to Ukraine are currently visa-free for most European, including the UK, USA, Canada and other nationals. If in doubt that this applies to you, DiviaMed can advise and assist you in obtaining a visa.
