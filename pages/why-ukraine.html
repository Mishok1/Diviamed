WHY UKRAINE

“Medical Tourism” - the seeking of medical treatment in another country is growing in popularity. There are any number of challenges that can prevent someone getting the treatment they need including: the high cost of medical services, particularly in the USA and Western Europe; the limited availability of treatments and specialists, equipment and medical specialists; legal limitations on certain medical procedures.

That is why selecting the right country offering the most appropriate medical help is essential. We can offer you at least six fundamental reasons why the answer to that question should be Ukraine:

Legislation– Ukraine has instituted comprehensive legislation which controls and governs both public and private health care providers and defines licensing, inspection and acceptable quality for medical services; this is particularly so in the field of reproductive medicine.

Training– building upon its traditional strong medical base Ukraine has developed international training partnerships to provide highly qualified medical professionals with the ability to apply the most modern and innovative therapies, particularly in reproductive and child-centered medicine, assuring you will receive the highest quality of medical services.

Equipment– in addition to personnel our medical partners have invested in the latest, most up-to-date medical equipment which combined with state-of-the-art techniques offers you unequalled potential to confront any health challenges you may face.

Availability– DiviaMed’s agreements with our medical partners guarantees that your treatment will be provided exactly when it suits you, with no waiting lists and to a schedule that is agreed with you in advance.

Cost– the costs of private medical treatment in the USA and Western Europe can be prohibitive for many people, even for many straightforward procedures. The lower cost of living in Ukraine results in significantly lower costs for medical treatment but also for accommodation, travel costs and all the other components that would make your trip affordable.

Language– the majority of our medical partners employ English speaking staff to ensure that you and they fully understand your medical needs. On the very few occasions when this is not the case, DiviaMed staff are available to provide interpretation and translation support.
